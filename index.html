CNSR Group

The CNSRG (Cyber and Network Security Research Group) mission is to perform innovative, relevant, and useful research that solves the Cyber security problems that arise because of the advancements in ICT by utilizing latest research methods and technologies.

What We Do

The primary objective of the group is to bring together expertise in education, research, and practice in the field of information, cyber and network security, and privacy.
